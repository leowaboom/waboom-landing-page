# Headline Formulas & Psychological Triggers

## A/B Test Ideas

Always suggest 2-3 headline variants:
- **Version A**: Problem-focused ("Stop losing leads at 2am")
- **Version B**: Outcome-focused ("Book 47 appointments this week")
- **Version C**: Curiosity ("The $50k mistake most businesses make")

---

## Headline Formulas by Psychological Trigger

### Curiosity Gap - Open a loop that demands closure
- "The [X] mistake that cost [Company] $[amount]"
- "Why [surprising group] are switching to [solution]"
- "The hidden reason your [process] is failing"
- "What [successful person] knows about [topic] that you don't"

### Fear/Loss Aversion - What they'll lose by not acting
- "Stop letting [villain] destroy your [valued thing]"
- "The [X] calls you're missing every [time period]"
- "How much is [problem] actually costing you?"

### Specificity - Concrete numbers create believability
- "How [Company] booked 47 appointments in 7 days"
- "The 3-step system for [outcome]"
- "$2.75 million: The cost of getting [X] wrong"

### Contrast/Transformation - Before vs After
- "From [bad state] to [good state] in [time]"
- "Why [old way] is dead (and what works now)"
- "[Old solution] vs [New solution]: The real difference"

### Challenge/Provocation - Poke their ego or assumptions
- "Most [audience] get this wrong"
- "The uncomfortable truth about [topic]"
- "What your [competitor/peer] isn't telling you"

### Secret/Insider - Exclusive knowledge
- "The [industry] secret that [big players] don't want you to know"
- "Inside the system that [impressive result]"
- "The feature that saved [clients] from [disaster]"

## Topic Ideas by Category

### Service-Based Businesses
- "Why your receptionist costs you $50k+ per year (and what to do about it)"
- "The 9 calls your team ignores every day (and the revenue they represent)"
- "How to automate 82% of inbound calls without losing customers"

### E-commerce
- "The abandoned cart email that recovered $127k in 30 days"
- "Why 68% of your customers leave before checkout"
- "The 3 product page tweaks that doubled conversion"

### Professional Services
- "Your competitors are answering leads in 5 minutes. You take 5 hours."
- "The follow-up sequence that tripled consultation bookings"
- "Why your best clients almost didn't hire you"

### General Business
- "Cold calling is dead. Here's what works now."
- "Your team is using AI wrong. Here's the fix."
- "The $100k skill gap: Why AI-literate employees earn more"

## Example Outputs

### Blog Hook
WRONG: "In today's rapidly evolving business landscape, AI is becoming increasingly important..."
RIGHT: "Your competitors are answering calls at 2am. You're not. Here's what that costs you."

### Social Post
WRONG: "Excited to share our latest AI solution!"
RIGHT: "Your best sales rep:
- Works 24/7
- Never calls in sick
- Handles 50 calls simultaneously
- Costs less than one salary

It's not a person. It's an AI voice agent.

We just deployed one for a client last month.
Week 1 results: 47 meetings booked. Zero human hours spent.

Still think AI can't sell?"
